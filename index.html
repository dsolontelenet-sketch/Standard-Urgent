Standard & Urgent

How to respond to inbound customer email, split by matter type. Follow the matter's lane top to bottom — Standard and Urgent are handled differently from the first message.

Standard Email Matter — acknowledge once, then track in Teams Urgent Matter — respond immediately, every time, per SLA
Lane 01

Standard Email Matter

Covers ordinary, non-urgent inquiries. The customer hears from you once at intake — after that, updates live in Teams, not in their inbox.

First Entry — Initial Inquiry

Step Action What to do
1 Send acknowledgment emailto the customer Let the customer know their inquiry was received and is waiting on the right person to pick it up.
Template · Acknowledgment
Dear [Mario], Thank you for reaching out to us. We acknowledge receipt of your inquiry and have noted the details you shared. Please rest assured that we are waiting for the appropriate person to handle your request, and we will provide you with an update as soon as possible. We appreciate your patience and understanding. Best regards, NetSTAR Support incompass_support@netstar-inc.com
2 Report in TeamsTeams Log the inquiry in Teams so the right person can pick it up.

Follow-up Entries

Step Action What to do
1 Report the update in TeamsTeams Post the latest status or development on the matter in Teams.
2 No reply neededNo customer reply The customer already received the acknowledgment at intake — do not send another email for follow-up updates.
Lane 02

Urgent Matter

Every entry on an urgent matter gets an immediate customer-facing response — there is no "first entry vs. follow-up" split like Standard. Speed and SLA language matter here.

All Entries

Step Action What to do
1 Send initial response immediatelyto the customer, every time Confirm the urgent request was received and is being handled under SLA. Don't wait — this goes out right away.
Template · Urgent acknowledgment
Dear [Name], This email is to confirm that we have your "urgent" support request. The NetSTAR support team has been made aware of the issue and we are now processing the request and will handle per the terms of SLA. We appreciate your cooperation, NetSTAR Support incompass_urgent@netstar-inc.com
2 Report in Teams as UrgentTeams · Urgent Log the matter in Teams and flag it as Urgent so it gets the right visibility and priority.
